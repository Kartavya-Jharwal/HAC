Financial Technical Analysis for Traders

Mar 5, 2026 • Hands-On Workshop

What You'll Learn

  • Technical analysis fundamentals and core principles
  • Chart patterns, candlesticks, and trend identification
  • Key indicators: moving averages, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands
  • AI-powered trading strategy and signal generation
  • Build your own technical analysis dashboard
  • Backtesting trading strategies with real data
  • Risk management and position sizing

Design Thinking Approach

This workshop applies systems thinking to market dynamics. You'll design data-driven trading systems that evolve with live market conditions — not static backtests on cherry-picked data. Expect feedback loops, real signals, and code that runs after the session ends.
