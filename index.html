What Is HAC?

Hult AI Collective is a technical buildspace where business students learn to ship AI tools using 2026-era frameworks—multi-agent systems, RAG pipelines, agentic workflows—without expensive infrastructure or CS prerequisites.

We're not a lecture series. We're not a networking club.

We're a common room where you show up, build something real, and leave with work you can use.

“Think Gryffindor common room meets Y Combinator office hours.”

Curated space. Low-stakes experimentation. Unreasonable hospitality.

What You'll Build

Practical Tools, Not Theory

→ Multi-Agent Systems

Build AI teams that research, write, and coordinate without you micromanaging every prompt.

→ Automation Workflows

Turn 3-hour manual tasks into 10-minute scripts. Import/export data, generate reports, scrape insights.

→ RAG Applications

Create AI that knows your business context—course materials, company docs, proprietary research—and answers intelligently.

Portfolio-ready projects. No fluff. Ship in weeks, not months.

How It Works

01

Monthly Workshops

2-hour hands-on build sessions. You ship a working prototype before you leave. No prerequisites required.

02

Co-Working Evenings

Bi-weekly drop-in sessions. Work on your project, debug with peers. Bring your laptop, stay as long as you want.

03

Common Room Culture

Music. Snacks. Space designed for focus and experimentation. HAC isn't just a calendar event—it's where you go when you want to build.

04

Trial Board Model

Want to run HAC with us? Join as a trial contributor, ship tasks for 2 weeks, earn your board role through execution. No interviews. Just ship.

Who This Is For

You'll thrive here if:

  • ✓You want to automate workflows, not just understand AI theory
  • ✓You're comfortable learning by building (and breaking things)
  • ✓You need portfolio-ready work for internships, startups, or jobs
  • ✓You're exploring devrel, cybersecurity, AI implementation roles beyond traditional business tracks

This probably isn't for you if:

  • ◦You want passive learning (lectures, panels, slide decks)
  • ◦You're collecting club memberships for your CV
  • ◦You need hand-holding through every line of code
  • ◦You expect fully polished, enterprise-grade infrastructure

HAC is for pragmatic operators and tech-curious explorers.
If you're here to ship, you're in the right place.

The Space

HAC sessions aren't held in generic lecture halls. We curate the environment: cafe seating, ambient music, thoughtful lighting, snacks on platters.
