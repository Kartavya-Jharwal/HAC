Who This Is For

You'll thrive here if:

  • You want to automate workflows, not just understand AI theory
  • You're comfortable learning by building (and breaking things)
  • You need portfolio-ready work for internships, startups, or jobs
  • You're exploring devrel, cybersecurity, AI implementation roles beyond traditional business tracks

This probably isn't for you if:

  • You want passive learning (lectures, panels, slide decks)
  • You're collecting club memberships for your CV
  • You need hand-holding through every line of code
  • You expect fully polished, enterprise-grade infrastructure

HAC is for pragmatic operators and tech-curious explorers. If you're here to ship, you're in the right place.
