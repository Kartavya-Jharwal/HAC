What We're Building

HAC operates as a hybrid incubator-studio for non-traditional technical talent.

Talent Pipeline

Business students learning to ship multi-agent systems, automation workflows, and RAG applications. Portfolio-ready work in 8 weeks.

Project Studio

Members building real tools—CRM automations for consultancies, research agents for VCs, compliance bots for legal teams. Not hypotheticals.

Ecosystem Hub

Cross-university buildspace. Hult students + UCL/Imperial/LSE non-CS participants. Open co-working evenings. Informal but serious.

We publish progress transparently:
  • Monthly demo videos (shipped projects, not slides)
  • GitHub repositories (open-source where possible)
  • Talent profiles (members open to opportunities)

Think YC office hours meets a gentleman's club. Drop by, see what's being built, steal our best people.
